Northern Territory


We have much to offer here in the Northern Territory. We are known for our rich culture and embracing our aboriginal history. You even have the chance to visit some aboriginal galleries with lots of art and images to see. If you want to go sightseeing, then we reccomend that you visit Uluru-Kata Tjuta national park. The parks most known attraction is of course Uluru, a 348 meter tall monolith (Showed to the right). Fun fact: The stone gets its beautiful red color from oxidation and metal-rust.
