Great Barrier Reef

The Great Barrier Reef is unique as it extends over 14 degrees of latitude, from shallow estuarine areas to deep oceanic waters. Within this vast expanse is a unique range of ecological communities, habitats and species – all of which make the Reef one of the most complex natural ecosystems in the world.

DayDream Island

Daydream Island resort offers Great Barrier Reef activities like circuit training in the rainforest and boot camps. The visitors can go for morning and evening jogs and take in the beauty of the mesmerising reef. Visitors can also enjoy a day of golf, helicopter tours and ocean rafting.
